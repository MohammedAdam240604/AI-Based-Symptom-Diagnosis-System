Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How does SymptoCheck work?

It uses machine learning to analyze your symptoms and predict possible diseases based on trained medical data.

❓ Can I use voice input?

Yes! Just click the 🎤 icon to speak your symptoms naturally.

❓ Is my data stored?

Yes, basic prediction logs (input & result) are stored locally in an SQLite database for history tracking.

❓ Should I rely on this for serious conditions?

No. This is a helpful assistant, not a replacement for a licensed healthcare provider.
